About

Tours are my passion:

I have been discovering the history of Austria with visitors for more than 10 years now. Each one of them puts people at the center and leads on a personal and unique journey to places that usually remain undiscovered.

Biography:

In 2012, studying history drew me to Graz and I immediately fell in love with the narrow streets, good food and especially the friendly people of this city. I have always had a fascination for the many small often inconspicuous details that are connecting our daily lives to times in the past: A beautifully forged door lock, a scratch that turns out to be a craftsman’s mark, or a beautiful facade that tells an unsuspected story… Above all at every place, there’s the stories of its people. There are many interesting things to learn and I enjoy sharing my exciting discoveries during guided tours. That’s why I took the Austria Guide training and became a state-certified guide in 2020. Since 2021 I am living in Vienna and from the beginning got completely under its spell. I have turned my passion into my profession here being responsible for the adult programs of cultural education at the Wien Museum.

Nevertheless, I can’t stop my expeditions with groups through Vienna and Graz and am already very much looking forward to the next tour with you!
